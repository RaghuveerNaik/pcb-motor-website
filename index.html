About Integrated Motion Systems

Pioneering the future of motor technology since 2010.

Our Story

Founded in 2010 by Anya Sharma, Integrated Motion Systems began as a small research lab focused on electromagnetic technologies. Our breakthrough came in 2015 when we developed the first commercially viable PCB motor.

Today, we're recognized as global leaders in compact motor solutions, with patents in 12 countries and products deployed across multiple industries.
